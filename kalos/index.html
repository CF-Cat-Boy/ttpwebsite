Kalos


The Kalos region is shaped like a five-pointed star and its geography and cities are based on those of France. Lumiose city, featured in the game Pokemon Legends Z-A, is in the central north of the region.

3000 years ago a great war broke out. The king of Kalos, AZ, was forced to send the Floette he inherited from his mother into battle, where it died. AZ couldn't stand losing Floette so he built a machine to resurrect it. He then turned the machine into the ultimate weapon, creating a devastating blast that ended the war, while sacrificing the lives of many pokémon. The explosion also gave both Floette and AZ eternal life. When Floette found out about this, it left AZ for a very long time.
